Prayers for Sri Lanka

Globetrek Overseas stands in solidarity with the people of Sri Lanka during this devastating flood crisis. We extend our heartfelt prayers and support to all affected communities.

Severe Floods Bring Widespread Devastation

Sri Lanka is currently facing one of its most challenging moments as continuous heavy rainfall has triggered severe flooding across multiple regions. Entire communities have been displaced, homes destroyed, and thousands of families are struggling to cope with the sudden impact.

Displaced Communities

Thousands of families have been forced to evacuate their homes as floodwaters inundate residential areas. Emergency shelters are at capacity as rescue operations continue around the clock.

Rescue Operations

Military personnel, emergency response units, and volunteers are working tirelessly to evacuate residents trapped in submerged areas. Access to many regions remains challenging due to damaged infrastructure.

Critical Services Affected

Essential services including electricity, transportation, and communication networks have been severely disrupted, hampering relief efforts and isolating affected communities.

About Globetrek Overseas

Globetrek Overseas is committed not only to supporting students in their international education journeys but also to showing solidarity with global communities in times of crisis. Humanity and empathy are at the core of our values.

We believe in the power of global citizenship and the responsibility we all share to support one another during difficult times, regardless of borders or backgrounds.
